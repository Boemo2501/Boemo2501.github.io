Andisabawel... Traveling

ANDISABAWEL is a term we use for craving something, eg. I sabaweli to travel. There is a saying that says "The world is a book and those who do not travel read only a page."
There is nothing precious like traveling,especiallly seeing a new place for the first time or returning to a favorite one.
People of all ages, from all around the world, go to foreign places for different reasons mainly, for work and spending some family time.
Whether by plane, train or ship, traveling is generally a enjoyable experience, at least for the people who can financially afford comfortable
and safe methods of travel. But it has more benefits than satisfying one's need to make money.

Never stop exploring the world
